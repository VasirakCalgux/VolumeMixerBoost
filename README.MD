# VolumeMixerBoost (Rootless & Volume Boost)

`VolumeMixerBoost` provides volume control for individual apps on iOS with an integrated boost feature. This is a fork of [brendonjkding/VolumeMixer](https://github.com/brendonjkding/VolumeMixer) updated for modern rootless jailbreaks and enhanced with volume amplification.


## Features
- **Individual App Volume**: Adjust volume for each app independently.
- **Volume Boost**: Boost audio up to 20x (2000%) for apps that are too quiet.
- **Rootless Support**: Fully compatible with Dopamine and other rootless jailbreaks.
- **Control Center Integration**: Access the volume mixer directly from your Control Center.
